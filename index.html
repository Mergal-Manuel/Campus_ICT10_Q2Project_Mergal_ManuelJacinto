About Us

O.B. Pasig University (OBPU) has raised young minds for 67 years with a focus on progressive but tradition-preserving education. Inspired by Montessorian values, we foster independence, creativity, and critical thinking among our students. Every hallway and classroom has seen the spark of curiosity among all the students here, and by which stem into their passion and careers. Here they mastered the cycle of work, the mastery and courage leadership takes, values that seep into every little decision your child makes. Here, education goes beyong the curriculum.
